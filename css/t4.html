Stanley Kubrick Ehibit Poster
San Francisco Contemporary Jewish Museum
This is a Poster I designed during my first semester at the California College of the Arts. We were tasked with designing an exhibit for a current or up-coming Bay Area event. I chose to create a poster for The San Francisco Contemporary Jewish Museum's June 2016 Stanley Kubrick Exhibition. I researched and drew from Kubrick's films and their promotional materials. The Typeface for the majority of the information on the poster is Futura, a typeface that Stanley Kubrick used almost exclusivly.
