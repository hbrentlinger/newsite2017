The Great Highway Skate Highway

Proposal to the City of San Francisco

This is a project that I worked on while taking Brett MacFadden’s graphic design 2 course at the California College of the Arts. The last was to design a public intervention, something out of the norm that would attract attention and add character to an area while fulfilling a need. I chose to once again work in my neighborhood, the Outer Sunset. The proposal that I came up with was to designate Lawton street as a “skate highway”. The intersecting roads would all be equipped with hand painted signs by local artists that read “yield to skateboards”.

Site design & development by Henry Brentlinger 2017
